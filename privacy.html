2. What we collect, and why

Account data

To create an account you provide an email address and password, or sign in with Apple (which shares your name and, optionally, a private relay email address). You can also use Eardium without an account in Guest mode, in which case we hold only an anonymous, randomly generated identifier with no personal information attached. Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Usage data

We store which sessions you've played, how many times, when you last played them, and which ones you've marked as favorites, so your library and listening history work across app launches. This is tied to your account (or your anonymous Guest identifier) and never shared or sold. Legal basis: performance of a contract / legitimate interest in providing the core feature (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f) GDPR).

Subscription & payment status

Eardium Pro is billed and processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment card details, billing address, or full purchase history — our systems only receive a signal of whether your subscription is currently active, so the app can unlock Pro content. Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Microphone access (not yet in use)

The app requests microphone permission on iOS in preparation for an upcoming voice-cloning feature that will let you hear rehearsal scripts read in your own voice. This feature is not active yet — no microphone audio is currently recorded, stored, or transmitted by the app. When it launches, any voice sample you choose to record will be uploaded securely and sent only to our voice-synthesis provider to build your personal voice clone, and you will be able to delete it at any time; we will update this policy and ask for your confirmation before that feature becomes active for you.

3. Who we share data with

We use a small number of service providers (processors) to run Eardium. None of them are permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

  • Supabase, Inc. (USA) — authentication, database, and file storage for the app's backend.
  • Apple Inc. — In-App Purchase billing, Sign in with Apple, and app distribution.
  • ElevenLabs, Inc. (USA) — text-to-speech synthesis used to produce the narrated audio in the app's content catalog, and (in future) any voice-cloning feature.

Where a provider is based outside the EU/EEA, the transfer is covered by that provider's certification under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and/or Standard Contractual Clauses under Art. 46 GDPR.

We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do not sell your personal information.

4. How long we keep your data

We keep account and usage data for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account inside the app, which removes your account and associated usage data. Some records may be retained briefly where required by law (e.g. billing records via Apple).

5. Children's privacy

Eardium is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.
