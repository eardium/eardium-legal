How do I cancel my Eardium Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple, not inside Eardium. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Eardium Pro → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of the period you already paid for.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open Eardium, go to the paywall screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you originally subscribed with.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app: Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and the usage data (favorites, play history) tied to it. See our Privacy Policy for details on what we store.

A session isn't playing, or audio is missing

First check your connection — first playback of a session downloads its audio. If it still fails, force-quit and reopen the app; if the problem continues, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll look into it.

I was charged but Pro content is still locked

Try Restore Purchases first (see above) — this re-checks your subscription status with Apple. If content is still locked after that, email us with your Apple ID email (not your password) and roughly when you subscribed.
